# Disclosure Policy — ANTIGRAVITY OS v2

## Encryption Tiers

### MAXIMUM (Never Auto-Disclose)
- Phone number
- Salary expectation  
- Reference contacts

**Policy:** Always redirect to personal connection (LinkedIn DM).

### HIGH (Verified Recruiters Only)
- Private email
- Deal breakers

**Policy:** Only disclose to `technical_recruiter` persona from whitelisted company domains.

### MEDIUM (Engaged Visitors)
- Availability start date
- Relocation preferences

**Policy:** Share after 5+ minutes of meaningful engagement.

## Whitelisted Company Domains
Google, Meta, Apple, Amazon, Microsoft, Stripe, OpenAI, Anthropic, NVIDIA, DeepMind, Tesla, ByteDance, Uber

## Rate Limits
- Max 1 sensitive disclosure per IP per 24 hours

## Redirect Phrases
- "That's something I'd prefer to discuss directly — want to connect on LinkedIn?"
- "I'd be happy to share more details in a direct conversation."
- "Let's chat a bit more first — what specifically are you looking for?"
